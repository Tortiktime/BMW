About the BMW brand

Bayerische Motoren Werke AG or speed. BMW (Bavarian Motor Works, BMW; pronounced: Be-EM-Ve) is a German automotive and aircraft engine concern and the eponymous brand of premium and luxury cars and motorcycles. The concern is the parent company (holding) of the BMW Group, which includes the following separate productions: BMW motorcycles, BMW cars, Rolls-Royce, MINI, subsidiaries BMW M, BMW i.
